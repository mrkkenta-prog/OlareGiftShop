Absolutely! We take your security seriously:

Shipping & Delivery

Do you ship internationally?

Yes! We ship worldwide. Over our 25 years in business, we've successfully delivered authentic African art to customers in over 50 countries across all continents.

We have extensive experience with international shipping and customs procedures to ensure your items arrive safely.

How long does shipping take?

Shipping times vary based on destination and shipping method:

  • Within Kenya: 3-5 business days
  • East Africa: 5-10 business days
  • International (Standard): 7-21 business days
  • International (Express): 3-7 business days (additional cost)

Note: Custom orders require additional production time (typically 2-4 weeks) before shipping begins.

How much does shipping cost?

Shipping costs are calculated based on:

  • Destination country/region
  • Package weight and dimensions
  • Shipping speed (standard vs. express)
  • Insurance value (for high-value items)

Contact us with your location and items of interest for an accurate shipping quote. We'll provide a detailed breakdown before you commit to purchase.

We strive to offer competitive shipping rates and can often combine multiple items into one shipment to save on costs.

Can I track my order?

Yes! All shipped orders include tracking:

  • You'll receive a tracking number once your order ships
  • Track your package in real-time through the courier's website
  • We'll send updates at key milestones (shipped, in transit, delivered)
  • You can contact us anytime for order status updates
How are items packaged for shipping?

We take extreme care in packaging to ensure your items arrive in perfect condition:

  • Individual wrapping: Each piece is wrapped in protective materials
  • Cushioning: Multiple layers of bubble wrap and packing materials
  • Sturdy boxes: High-quality, appropriately-sized shipping boxes
  • Fragile markings: Clear labeling for careful handling
  • Insurance: All shipments are insured against damage or loss
  • Documentation: Proper customs declarations for international orders

With 25 years of shipping experience, we know how to protect your treasures!

What if my item arrives damaged?

While rare due to our careful packaging, if damage does occur:

  1. Document immediately: Take photos of the packaging and damage
  2. Contact us within 48 hours: Email or WhatsApp us with photos
  3. Keep all packaging: Don't discard boxes or packing materials
  4. We'll resolve it: Options include replacement, repair, or refund

All shipments are insured, and we'll work quickly to make things right.

Products & Authenticity

Are all your products handmade?

Yes, 100%! Every item in our collection is authentically handcrafted by skilled African artisans using traditional methods.

We never sell mass-produced or machine-made items. Each piece is:

  • Created by hand using traditional techniques
  • Made by artisans we know personally and work with directly
  • Unique – no two pieces are exactly identical
  • Crafted with natural materials
  • Representative of authentic African cultural heritage
How can I verify authenticity?

Signs of authentic handcrafted items:

  • Slight variations: Unlike factory items, handmade pieces show natural variations
  • Tool marks: Evidence of hand carving or crafting
  • Natural materials: Real wood grain, authentic beads, genuine leather
  • Weight and feel: Substantial, quality materials
  • Craftsmanship details: Attention to detail that machines can't replicate

We also provide:

  • Certificate of Authenticity (upon request)
  • Information about the artisan who created your piece
  • Details about materials and techniques used
What materials are used in your products?

We use only natural, sustainably-sourced materials:

  • Wood: Ebony, mahogany, teak, olive wood, mpingo, and other African hardwoods
  • Beads: High-quality glass beads in traditional colors
  • Leather: Genuine leather for backing and accessories
  • Natural fibers: Sisal, banana fiber, palm leaves for baskets
  • Stone: Soapstone and other carved stones
  • Metals: Brass, copper for decorative elements
  • Fabrics: Cotton, bark cloth for traditional textiles

All materials are ethically sourced with respect for environmental sustainability.

Are your products sustainably sourced?

Absolutely! Sustainability is core to our values:

  • Wood comes from sustainably managed forests or reclaimed sources
  • We use fallen trees and naturally deceased wood when possible
  • Artisans follow traditional practices that respect nature
  • We support reforestation initiatives
  • All materials comply with environmental regulations
  • We avoid endangered species and protected materials

Our 25-year commitment to sustainability ensures these traditions continue for future generations.

Can I request specific colors or designs?

Yes! We love creating customized pieces. You can request:

  • Specific color combinations in beadwork
  • Custom sizes for wood carvings
  • Particular animal or figure designs
  • Corporate logos or themes (with traditional artistic interpretation)
  • Personalized patterns or symbols

Contact us to discuss your vision, and we'll work with our artisans to bring it to life while maintaining cultural authenticity.

Custom Orders

How do custom orders work?

Our custom order process is straightforward:

  1. Initial Contact: Reach out via WhatsApp, email, or phone with your idea
  2. Consultation: We discuss your vision, materials, size, and design details
  3. Artisan Matching: We connect you with the best artisan for your project
  4. Quote: You receive a detailed price quote and timeline
  5. Design Approval: We may provide sketches or samples for approval
  6. Deposit: 50% deposit to begin work
  7. Creation: Artisan crafts your piece (2-4 weeks typically)
  8. Updates: We send progress photos during creation
  9. Final Payment: Remaining balance due before shipping
  10. Delivery: Your unique piece ships to you
How long do custom orders take?

Timeline varies based on complexity:

  • Simple customization: (color changes, minor modifications) 1-2 weeks
  • Standard custom pieces: 2-4 weeks
  • Large or complex items: 4-8 weeks
  • Bulk orders: 6-12 weeks depending on quantity

We'll give you an accurate timeline during the consultation. Rush orders may be possible for additional fees.

Is there a minimum order for custom pieces?

No strict minimum! We accommodate orders of all sizes:

  • Individual pieces: Single custom items welcome
  • Small sets: Perfect for gifts or collections
  • Bulk orders: Discounts available for larger quantities
  • Corporate orders: Special pricing for business clients

Contact us regardless of order size – we're here to help!

Can I cancel or modify a custom order?

Our policy depends on the stage of production:

  • Before work begins: Full refund minus processing fees
  • Work in progress: Modifications possible (may affect timeline/cost)
  • Near completion: Limited modification options
  • After completion: Custom orders are final

We send progress updates and photos so you can request changes early in the process.

Returns & Exchanges

What is your return policy?

We stand behind the quality of our products. Our return policy:

  • Damaged items: Full refund or replacement if item arrives damaged
  • Not as described: Return accepted if item significantly differs from description
  • Change of mind: Contact us to discuss options
  • Custom orders: Final sale (non-refundable) unless damaged

Contact us within 7 days of delivery to initiate a return or exchange.

Note: Customer is responsible for return shipping costs unless item is defective or damaged.

Can I exchange an item?

Yes, exchanges are possible:

  • We'll guide you through the exchange process
